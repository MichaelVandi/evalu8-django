Why use our site?

  • No Corporate Interference: You can trust evalu8 to give you reviews left by REAL humans and scores that are completely unaffected by corporate agencies. We do not ask restaurants for money to include their business on our site and we won’t accept money from restaurants to manipulate reviews or scores. The data you get from evalu8 is 100% untampered with. We live for data accuracy!

  • Free Always: You deserve to get ratings, reviews, and scores. It is your data and we believe you should not pay for it. evalu8 will be free, always!

  • No Ads: Ads aren’t cool.

  • Extensively Open Source: Evalu8 is built on open-source data. It makes sense that we commit our resources to the development of the open-source society. The entire source code of this project is publicly available on the developer’s GitHub. Access it via this link.

  • Our story: We are not a group of thousands of developers, we do not have the numbers or the finances. In fact, we are exactly just one developer plus you, our customer, it’s plausible you might be our only visitor, but what we do pride ourselves in is the data we are providing you and the satisfaction we get from knowing real people like you are actually using that data to make informed decisions about where to get their next meal. That is what inspires us, and every day, when one cool feature pops up in our minds, it should only take one late night of coding before you see it on our platform the next morning.
